Cookies Policy

Learn about how Oshie Pruning Services uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience, understand usage patterns, and improve our services.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) by websites that you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, remember your preferences, and provide information to website owners.

These cookies store information about your browsing behavior or device to help us personalize your experience and collect analytics data.

Types of Cookies We Use

At Oshie Pruning Services, we use several categories of cookies, including:

1. Essential Cookies

These cookies are vital for the operation of our website and to provide you with core services. Without these cookies, certain website features may become unavailable or may not function properly.

2. Performance and Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect anonymous information about how visitors use our website. They help us understand user interaction, page visits, and identify areas for improvement.

We use tools like Google Analytics to gather aggregated data to enhance user experience while respecting your privacy.

3. Functional Cookies

Functional cookies remember choices you have made (such as language preferences or login details) to provide a more personalized experience on subsequent visits.

4. Advertising and Targeting Cookies

We may use cookies to deliver relevant ads based on your browsing behavior. These cookies help measure the effectiveness of our advertising campaigns. We do not share personally identifiable information with advertisers without your explicit consent.

How We Use Cookies

Cookies enable us to:

  • Recognize returning visitors and remember your preferences and settings for faster navigation.
  • Understand which pages are most popular and improve our website structure and content.
  • Customize content and offers to help you find services more relevant to your interests.
  • Ensure security by detecting suspicious activities or unauthorized access attempts.
  • Measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns and tailor advertisements accordingly.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third parties that provide services on our behalf, such as analytics providers, advertising networks, and social media platforms.

These third parties may use cookies to collect information about your online activities over time and across different websites, enabling targeted advertising or performance measurement.

We do not control these third-party cookies, and their use is governed by their own privacy and cookie policies. We encourage you to review their policies to understand how your data is used.

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie banner prompting you to accept or decline cookies. Your choice will be stored in your browser for future visits to respect your preferences.

If you do not consent to cookies, only essential cookies necessary for the website to function will be used.

You can change your cookie preferences or withdraw your consent at any time by clearing your browser cookies or adjusting your browser settings.
