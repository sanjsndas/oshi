Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We provide pruning services throughout New York City and surrounding boroughs. Contact us to check availability in your neighborhood.

How do I book a pruning service?

You can contact us via phone, email, or fill out the contact form on this page. We will schedule a free consultation and provide a detailed estimate.

Are your arborists certified?

Yes, all our arborists are trained professionals certified by recognized horticultural and arboricultural organizations.

Do you provide emergency pruning services?

We offer emergency storm damage pruning. Contact us immediately if you have urgent tree damage concerns.

What are your pricing structures?

Pricing depends on the scope and complexity of the job. Our pricing guide is available on the Services page. We provide detailed estimates after consultation.

Is your service environmentally friendly?

Absolutely! We use eco-conscious pruning methods and dispose of green waste responsibly to minimize environmental impact.

How do I prepare for a pruning appointment?

Please ensure safe access to trees or garden areas, keep pets indoors, and inform us of any special concerns ahead of time.

Can you advise on tree health beyond pruning?

Yes, we offer assessments and recommendations for overall tree health and maintenance. Just ask during your consultation.
